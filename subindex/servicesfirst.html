We provide sanitary pad vending machine, automatic water tank, remote control curtain, remote control project curtain, automatic traditional alcohol maker, smoke filtration in Nepal etc.

In today's fast-paced world, convenience and automation are key elements that enhance the quality of life. Recognizing this need, we are excited to introduce our latest range of innovative in-house products, designed to make everyday tasks simpler and more efficient. From sanitary pad vending machines to automatic water tanks, our products cater to various aspects of modern living, ensuring comfort, convenience, and peace of mind.

Our sanitary pad vending machines are a discreet and practical solution for maintaining hygiene. Perfect for homes, offices, and public restrooms, these machines provide easy access to sanitary products, ensuring that necessities are always within reach. The vending machines are designed for ease of use and can be installed in various locations, making them an essential addition to any environment that values hygiene and convenience.

The system uses advanced sensors and control mechanisms to monitor water levels and manage refills automatically, providing an uninterrupted water supply while conserving resources. This smart solution not only saves time and effort but also contributes to water conservation efforts.

For those who appreciate smart home technology, our remote control curtains and project curtains offer a blend of luxury and functionality. These curtains can be operated with a simple click of a button, allowing you to control the ambiance of your home effortlessly. Whether you're looking to adjust the light for a movie night or ensure privacy with ease, these remote control solutions offer unmatched convenience and style.

The traditional alcohol maker combines age-old brewing techniques with modern automation, enabling users to create high-quality beverages at home with minimal effort. Meanwhile, our advanced smoke filtration systems ensure a cleaner, healthier living environment by effectively removing smoke and pollutants from the air. These products not only enhance lifestyle but also ensure a healthier and more enjoyable home experience.

By integrating these innovative products into your home, you can transform daily routines into seamless, enjoyable experiences. Our commitment to quality and convenience drives us to continually develop solutions that meet the evolving needs of modern households.
